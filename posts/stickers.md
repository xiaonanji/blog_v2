<section>
  <h2>Markdown 语法速查</h2>
  <div class="stickers">
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>标题</strong></p>
      <p><code># 一级标题</code></p>
      <p><code>## 二级标题</code></p>
      <p><code>### 三级标题</code></p>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>文本样式</strong></p>
      <p><code>&#42;斜体&#42;</code> 或 <code>&#95;斜体&#95;</code></p>
      <p><code>&#42;&#42;粗体&#42;&#42;</code> 或 <code>&#95;&#95;粗体&#95;&#95;</code></p>
      <p><code>&#126;&#126;删除线&#126;&#126;</code></p>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>列表</strong></p>
      <p><code>- 无序列表</code></p>
      <p><code>&#42; 也是无序</code></p>
      <p><code>1. 有序列表</code></p>
      <p><code>2. 第二项</code></p>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>引用与代码</strong></p>
      <code>&gt; 引用内容</code>
      <p/>
      <code>&#96;行内代码&#96;</code>
      <p/>
      <code>&#96;&#96;&#96;代码块&#96;&#96;&#96;</code>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>链接与图片</strong></p>
      <code>&#91;链接文字&#93;(url)</code>
      <p/>
      <code>!&#91;图片描述&#93;(url)</code>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>表格</strong></p>
      <code>&#124; 列1 &#124; 列2 &#124;</code>
      <p/>
      <code>&#124; --- &#124; --- &#124;</code>
      <p/>
      <code>&#124; 内容1 &#124; 内容2 &#124;</code>
    </div>

    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>换行</strong></p>
      <p>如何单换行不显示为换行，使用两个换行(隔一行)执行markdown中的换行</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section>
  <h2>Multi-cursor mode</h2>
  <div class="stickers">
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Add cursor with mouse drag</strong></p>
      <p><code>Shift+Alt+left drag</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Add by clicking</strong></p>
      <p><code>Ctrl+click</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Select all occurrences of selection</strong></p>
      <p><code>Ctrl+Shift+L</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Select next occurrences of selection</strong></p>
      <p><code>Ctrl+D</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Multi-Cursor on beginning of line:</strong></p>
      <p>
      1. Place cursor at the beginning of the first line. 
      2. Hold <code>Alt + Shift</code> and click at the beginning of the last line. 
      3. Or hold <code>Alt + Shift and press Down Arrow</code> repeatedly
      </p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Multi-Cursor on beginning of line:</strong></p>
      <p>
      1. Select all the lines you want to edit. 
      2. Press <code>Alt + Shift + I</code> — this places a cursor at the end of each selected line. 
      3. Press Home to move all cursors to the beginning
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

  <h2>MacOS</h2>
  <div class="stickers">
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Toggle full screen:</strong></p>
      <p><code>Ctrl+Alt+F</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Screenshot</strong></p>
      <p>Entire screen: <code>Shift+Cmd+3</code></p>
      <p>Selected: <code>Shift+Cmd+4</code></p>
      <p>To clipboard: Hold <code>Ctrl</code> while taking the shot</p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Choose input languages:</strong></p>
      <p><code>Ctrl+Space</code></p>
    </div>
    <div class="sticker">
      <p><strong>Terminal command</strong></p>
      <p>Kill: <code>Ctrl+C</code></p>
      <p>Suspend: <code>Ctrl+D</code></p>
    </div>
  </div>
